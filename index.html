About us!

We Dunnuraine for Trading and Agencies. Founded in 2004 by its chairman Mr. Anwar Abdo Al Haj and located in Sana’a the capital city of the Republic of Yemen, we are a renowned and fast growing distributor of pharmaceuticals and cosmetics representing a number of international companies in the local market. Our management always endeavours to have dealings with only the best in class suppliers and manufacturers so that the Yemeni consumer can avail of quality medications at very affordable prices. We are committed to achieving this goal through earnest dedication to the cause and adoption of ethical business practices.

PARTNERSHIP APPROACH

Dunnuraine Trading has adopted a unique approach to the market that has resulted in distribution agreements with several major multinational pharmaceutical companies and their subsidiaries. Dunnuraine Trading has followed a partnership approach to the market which is unique in a market where the distributor traditionally dictates terms to its suppliers and invests little of its own resources, Dunnuraine Trading has always maintained a close contact with its suppliers, participates in the strategic planning, business development, budgeting, problem solving, and any other related areas that potentially affect both parties.

NEW PRODUCT REGISTRATION

Dunnuraine Trading has established a Registration Department whose sole mission is to dedicate maximum effort, in conjunction with the manufacturer, towards registering new products in a timely manner.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to become a transnational pharmaceutical company through the development of our distribution network and introduction of a wide portfolio of branded and generic products in the Yemeni market.
